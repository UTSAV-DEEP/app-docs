# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 28, 2026**

Automated Instrumental Music ("we", "our", or "us") provides this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection & Permissions
Our app is designed to respect your privacy and operates with minimal requirements:
- **No Special Permissions:** The app does not require access to your contacts, location, microphone, or camera.
- **Personal Data:** We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- **Local Storage:** App data (favorites, custom genres) and generated music are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your compositions or app data to our servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services (Advertising)
We use third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) to support the app. These services are the **only** components that may collect data:
- **Device Identifiers:** To serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
- **Usage Data:** To track ad performance and prevent fraud.

Please refer to the [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads) for more details.

## 3. Data Storage
All app data is stored in an encrypted format locally on your device.

## 4. Visual Content & AI
All category images and visual assets within the app are AI-generated. Any pictures featuring human faces or figures are entirely synthetic and do not represent any real individual. These images do not violate the privacy rights of any person. Any resemblance to real persons, living or dead, is purely coincidental.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
